Clean & Maintain 🧼

Keeping your computer clean and well-maintained helps it run better and last longer.

Physical cleaning tips:

  • Use a soft, dry cloth to wipe your screen and keyboard.
  • Use a small brush or air duster to clean between keys and vents.
  • Never spray liquids directly on your computer.

Digital maintenance tips:

  • Remove unused files and programs.
  • Run Disk Cleanup (search for it in the Start menu).
  • Update Windows and software regularly.
  • Restart your computer at least once a week.
