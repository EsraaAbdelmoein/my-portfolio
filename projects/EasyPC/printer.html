Use a Printer 🖨️

Follow these simple steps to connect and use a printer with your computer.

Connect your printer:

  1. Plug the printer into a power source and turn it on.
  2. Connect the printer to your computer using a USB cable or Wi-Fi (check the printer's instructions).
  3. Wait for your computer to detect the printer.
  4. Install any drivers if prompted.

Print a document:

  1. Open the document you want to print.
  2. Click “File” > “Print”.
  3. Select your printer from the list.
  4. Click “Print” and wait for the document to finish printing.
