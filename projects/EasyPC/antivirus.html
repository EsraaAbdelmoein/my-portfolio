Stay Protected 🛡️

Antivirus software helps protect your computer from viruses and threats.

Basic protection steps:

  1. Install antivirus software like Windows Defender or Avast.
  2. Open the antivirus and click "Scan" to check for viruses.
  3. Follow the instructions to remove any threats.
  4. Keep your antivirus software updated.

Safety tips:

  • Don’t open unknown email attachments.
  • Don’t click suspicious links.
  • Use strong passwords for your accounts.
