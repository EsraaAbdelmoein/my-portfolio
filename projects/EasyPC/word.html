Write Documents ✍️

Here is how to use Microsoft Word to create simple documents like letters or notes.

Steps to start writing:

  1. Click the Microsoft Word icon on your desktop or Start menu.
  2. Choose "Blank Document".
  3. Start typing your message or note.
  4. Click “File” > “Save As” to save your document.

Tips:

  • Use the bold, italic, and underline buttons to format text.
  • Use bullet points for lists.
  • Save your work regularly!
