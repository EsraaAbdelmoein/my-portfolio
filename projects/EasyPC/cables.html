This guide will show you how to connect external devices like a mouse, keyboard, or USB stick to your computer.

Common connections:

  • USB: Used for flash drives, printers, phones, etc.
  • HDMI: Used to connect your computer to a TV or monitor.
  • Audio Jack: For headphones or speakers.

How to connect:

  1. Find the correct port on your computer (USB, HDMI, etc.).
  2. Gently plug in the device.
  3. Wait a few seconds. Most devices are recognized automatically.
  4. If needed, install any drivers or software the device asks for.
