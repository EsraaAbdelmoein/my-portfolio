Frequently Asked Questions ❓

💾 How do I save a file?

Click “File” > “Save As”, give it a name, choose where to save it, then click “Save”.

🔐 How do I create a password?

Go to Settings > Accounts > Sign-in options, then click “Add” under Password.

🌐 Why is my internet not working?

Make sure Wi-Fi is on. Restart your router and reconnect if needed.

🖨️ My printer won’t print. What can I try?

Check the connection, power, paper, and ink. Try restarting the printer.

📁 How can I find my files?

Open File Explorer and check the "Documents" or "Downloads" folders.

🔇 There's no sound on my computer. What do I do?

Make sure the volume is up and the speakers or headphones are plugged in.

⚠️ My screen is frozen. What should I do?

Try pressing Ctrl + Alt + Delete and restart the computer if needed.

📧 How do I check my email?

Open your web browser, go to your email provider (like Gmail), and sign in.

🛑 How do I close a program?

Click the “X” in the top-right corner of the window, or use Alt + F4.

💡 What is a browser?

A browser is a program (like Chrome) that lets you access the internet.

💣 How do I remove a virus?

Use antivirus software to scan your computer and remove threats.

🧹 How do I clean up my computer?

Delete unused files and programs, and run Disk Cleanup from the Start menu.

🔄 How do I restart my computer?

Click Start > Power > Restart.

⏳ My computer is slow. What can I do?

Restart your PC, close unused programs, and check for updates.

🔎 How can I search for something online?

Open a browser, go to Google, and type what you’re looking for in the search box.
