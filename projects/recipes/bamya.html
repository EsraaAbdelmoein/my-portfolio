Okra Stew (Bamia)

Rich Tomato Okra Delight

Bamia is a traditional Egyptian stew made with tender green okra simmered in a flavorful tomato sauce, often served with lamb or beef. It’s a cozy, satisfying dish perfect for family meals.

Ingredients:

  • 500g okra (fresh or frozen)
  • 300g beef or lamb (cubed)
  • 1 large onion (chopped)
  • 4 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 3 cups tomato puree
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • Salt, pepper, and ground coriander to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a pot, heat the oil and sauté onions until golden. Add the meat cubes and cook until browned.
  2. Add garlic and tomato paste, stir for a minute, then add tomato puree and spices.
  3. Simmer until the meat is tender (about 45–60 minutes).
  4. Add okra and cook for another 20 minutes until soft.
  5. Serve hot with white rice or baladi bread.
