Golden Crunch with Sweet Syrup

Kunafa is a beloved Middle Eastern dessert made with shredded phyllo dough, soaked in sweet syrup and filled with cream, cheese, or nuts. Crispy, golden, and irresistible!

Ingredients:

  • 500g kunafa dough (shredded phyllo)
  • 200g melted butter or ghee
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp rose water or vanilla
  • 1 cup cream, sweet cheese, or nuts (for filling)

Instructions:

  1. Mix shredded dough with melted butter until fully coated.
  2. Press half of it into a greased pan.
  3. Add filling (cream, cheese, or nuts), then top with remaining dough.
  4. Bake at 180°C (350°F) for 30–40 minutes until golden.
  5. Meanwhile, make syrup: boil sugar, water, lemon juice for 10 minutes, add rose water at the end.
  6. Pour cool syrup over hot kunafa. Serve warm!
