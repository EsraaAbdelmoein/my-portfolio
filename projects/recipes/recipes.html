Molokhia

A savory green soup made with jute leaves, garlic, and coriander. Usually served with rice and meat.

Okra (Bamia)

A tomato-based stew with okra and tender lamb or beef, rich in flavor and heritage.

Fattah with Meat & Rice

Layered rice, fried bread, and garlic tomato sauce topped with meat chunks – a festive favorite.

Moussaka

Eggplant slices baked with spiced minced meat and tomato sauce. Delicious and satisfying.

Meat Goulash

Thin layers of phyllo filled with spiced minced beef, crispy outside and juicy inside.

Bechamel Pasta

Macaroni baked with creamy bechamel sauce and seasoned ground meat – Egyptian comfort food.

Cheese Goulash

Crunchy goulash sheets filled with a soft blend of white Egyptian cheeses.

Stuffed Grape Leaves

Delicate vine leaves stuffed with herbed rice mix, slow-cooked with lemon and broth.

Stuffed Cabbage

Cabbage leaves wrapped around rice and herbs, layered and steamed to perfection.

Basbousa

A semolina cake soaked in syrup and often topped with coconut or almonds.

Kunafa

Shredded phyllo dough layered with cream or nuts, baked and soaked in sweet syrup.

Qatayef

Stuffed mini pancakes filled with cream or nuts, deep-fried and soaked in syrup.

Eid Cookies (Kahk)

Powdered sugar cookies filled with dates, nuts, or agameya. Traditional Eid treat.

Feteer Meshaltet

Flaky layered Egyptian pastry, served plain or with sweet or savory fillings.

Zalabia

Golden fried dough balls dipped in syrup or dusted with sugar. Sweet and crispy.

Kofta

Minced meat skewers seasoned with spices and grilled to juicy perfection.

Om Ali

Warm Egyptian bread pudding with nuts and milk, baked until golden and comforting.

Koshari

Egypt's national dish made with rice, lentils, pasta, chickpeas, topped with crispy onions and tomato sauce.
