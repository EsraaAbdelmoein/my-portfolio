Crispy Phyllo Layers with Savory Meat

Egyptian meat goulash is a savory baked dish made of thin phyllo pastry filled with seasoned minced meat, cut into squares and baked until golden and crispy.

Ingredients:

  • 1 package phyllo pastry sheets
  • 300g minced beef
  • 1 onion (finely chopped)
  • Salt, pepper, cinnamon, and nutmeg
  • 3 tablespoons melted butter or ghee
  • 1 egg + 1 cup milk (for topping mixture)

Instructions:

  1. In a pan, sauté onion then add minced beef. Cook and season with spices. Let it cool.
  2. Grease a baking dish. Layer half the phyllo sheets, brushing each with butter.
  3. Spread the meat filling evenly.
  4. Cover with the remaining phyllo sheets, brushing each with butter.
  5. Cut into squares, then pour the egg-milk mixture on top.
  6. Bake at 180°C (350°F) for 30–40 minutes until golden and crisp.
