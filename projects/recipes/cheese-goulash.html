Crispy Phyllo with Creamy Cheese

This Egyptian cheese goulash is a savory phyllo pastry filled with a creamy mix of cheeses, baked until perfectly golden and flaky. A delicious light dish or snack!

Ingredients:

  • 1 package phyllo pastry sheets
  • 200g Egyptian white cheese (or feta)
  • 100g mozzarella (optional, for extra meltiness)
  • 1 egg
  • Black seeds or parsley (optional)
  • 3 tablespoons melted butter or ghee
  • 1/2 cup milk

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix the cheeses, egg, and optional herbs.
  2. Grease a baking dish. Layer half the phyllo sheets, brushing each with butter.
  3. Spread the cheese mixture evenly.
  4. Cover with the remaining phyllo sheets, brushing with butter between layers.
  5. Mix milk with a bit of leftover egg and pour on top.
  6. Bake at 180°C (350°F) for 30 minutes until golden and set.
