About Me

Hello! I'm Esraa Abdelmoein, a criminal law graduate who found her passion in the world of technology and programming. I graduated from university as a criminal lawyer, and over time, my love for computers and technology inspired me to shift my path toward IT.

I completed the IT Analyst Program at NPower Canada, where I gained hands-on experience in IT support, networking, and troubleshooting. This program helped build a strong technical foundation and encouraged me to pursue more advanced programming skills.

Currently, I’m studying IT Programming and learning languages like Java, C, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I bring with me key skills from my legal background—like critical thinking, attention to detail, and problem-solving—that perfectly support my growth as a developer.
